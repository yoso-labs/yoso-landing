We are a deep-tech collective dedicated to building what matters, openly.

In an era of relentless AI hype and digital-only "wrappers," Yoso Labs exists to ground high-level intelligence into the physical and mathematical world. We aren't here to build another chatbot. We are here to build artifacts.

01 // Implementation

Artifacts Over Abstracts

Theory is the foundation, but a system is the goal. We don’t stop at the paper; we build the code, the hardware, and the implementation. If it doesn’t do something tangible, it isn’t a Yoso project.

02 // Focus

The Anti-Hype Filter

We ignore the noise. Our focus is dictated by genuine fascination, not by the shifting tides of the "AI hype cycle." We work on problems that are intellectually demanding and critically important, regardless of whether they are currently deemed "cool" or popular.

03 // Openness

Radical Transparency

The "Lab" is a glass box. We live-stream our failures, open-source our successes, and document the "messy middle" of the learning process. Our value isn't in a proprietary secret, but in our verifiable ability to execute.

04 // Process

Excellence Over Perfection

We don’t wait for total mastery to begin. We seek excellence through iteration, acknowledging that in deep tech, it is impossible to know everything. We value the courage to learn in public over the safety of staying within our comfort zones.

05 // Direction

Thematic Seasons

We are domain-agnostic but focus-driven. Every year, we set a Thematic Compass to guide our collective energy—from Sustainability to Robotics to Smart Infrastructure. We build the future we actually want to live in.

06 // Community

The Collective

Yoso is a bridge. We invite researchers, hackers, and engineers to step out of their silos and build "weird" things that don't fit in a corporate roadmap or a traditional thesis.
